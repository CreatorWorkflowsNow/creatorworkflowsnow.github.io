4.1 Create a Workspace

Overview‚Äã

Next you will create and configure a Workspace experience for the Fulfiller users of the application.

Instructions‚Äã

  1. Return to the App Engine Studio browser tab.
  1. On the application homepage, click the Experience tab.
  1. Click +Add.
  1. Click the Workspace tile to create a workspace to help users manage and fulfill requests sent to them.
  1. Click on the Begin button to begin the Workspace Builder wizard.
  1. Leave the default values and click Continue.

  1. Set Primary table and Secondary tables like below and click on the Continue button.

    Field NameField Value
    Primary tableTelework Case
    Secondary tablesArrangement, Justification

  1. Your new Workspace experience is completed. Click on the Edit button.
  1. You are now directed to the Workspace Builder. Click on the Get started button.
  1. Your Workspace Builder view should look like below.

    The view below might look slightly different depending on your screen resolution.

The Workspace Builder for App Engine allows you to manage dashboards, build lists, and customize record pages for workspaces created in App Engine Studio. App Engine licensed customers enjoy exclusive access to this premium feature.
