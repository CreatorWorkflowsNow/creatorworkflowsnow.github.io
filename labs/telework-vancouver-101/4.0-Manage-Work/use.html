We need to reassign his work to another case worker. (It is hard to do when the work is managed via emails and spreadsheets!)

  1. Let's find all the open cases assigned to Luke Wilson

    1. Click the List icon.

    4. Click the button next to "Luke Wilson".
    5. click "Show Matching".

  1. Let's assign Luke's cases to Andrew Och

    1. Select all rows
    2. Click Edit
    3. Find the Assigned to field and type and
    4. Select "Andrew Och"
    5. Click Update.
