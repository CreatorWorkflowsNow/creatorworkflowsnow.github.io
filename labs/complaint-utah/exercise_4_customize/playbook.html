Configure Complaints Playbook using Process Automation Designer

  1. First, end any impersonation you may have set in a previous lab.

  1. Change your Application scope by clicking on the globe icon in the top right of the screen and choosing CSM Workspace from the list

  1. Then in the Filter Navigator, enter Customer Service and choose My Cases under Cases

  1. Open case number CS0009003 by clicking on the case number

  1. Click on the hamburger icon and choose Configure > Form Design from the list

  1. In Form Designer, change the view (second dropdown) to Workspace.

  1. Find the Requires Investigation field using the filter on the Fields tab

  1. Drag and drop the field under Needs attention in the right column

  1. Click Save

  1. To test the new configuration, open the CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace, open a case and see the new field

Excellent! Now that we have our form configured, we can move on to creating our Investigation app in App Engine Studio!
